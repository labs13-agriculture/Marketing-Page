Effortless CRM

Beautifully simple tools made for Tieme Ndo to improve the productivity of both their organization and clients.

Features

The Tieme Ndo CRM was designed prioritizing simplicity, performance, and ease of use. Here is some additional filler content to see what the page looks like with more content.

Dark Mode

Manage installments, inventory, or transactions at any time of day.

Inventory

Now you can see your whole inventory in one place, and easily add or update your data.

Transactions

Complete client transaction histories are now two clicks away. Transactions even update your inventory.

Installments

Quickly see who is due for an installment or enter a successfully paid installment.

Search

Instant access to your entire client base by name, location or both.

Lead Support

Search and categorize clients based on their lead potential.
