Tieme Ndo CRM is an app commissioned to help Tieme Ndo keep track of their clients. Consisting of Farmers, Retailers, and Organizations, their client-base is expansive and difficult to track without the proper tools. Without the help of this CRM, Tieme Ndo staff were relying on paper records or, in some cases, attempting to commit their client interactions to memory.

Easily Manage Clients!

Client data has never been so easy to manipulate! Quickly update or delete client demographic data, add new transactions, or record installment payments, all from the same place!

Keep Track of your Inventory

Now you can see your whole inventory in one place, and easily add or update your data!

Find a client!

Quickly view all clients with the click of a button, or enter some information into the search fields to narrow down the results.
